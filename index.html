What is this?

Every week, we gather the incredible animal photos you share with us on our social media platforms. We carefully curate the top 5 submissions that we believe are the most outstanding, showcasing them here for everyone to enjoy.

How can I participate?

Simply head over to any of our social media platforms and tag us in your post using #PhotoWeek in the description.

Rules

  • No Photoshop! Photos must be natural and not tinkered with.
  • Photo has to be off a wild animal.(no pets)
  • You can submit up to 2 photos per week. Choose wisely!
    (If you submit more than 2 you will be disqualified for that week)
