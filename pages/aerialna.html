Welcome to our animal catalog, here you will find all the animals related to previously selected continent and category. To get started, find an animal you like and click on its card. The card will expand and show more information on the animal. When you're ready, you can shrink the card back down by clicking anywhere inside it, or you can continue on and expand even more cards. Have fun learning about all the unusual animal that are out there in the world!
